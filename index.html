Clinical Decision Support

All calculations are based on bundled reference datasets and operate fully offline.

Core assessment tools

  • AAP and NICE guideline selection
  • Gestational age-based threshold curves
  • Neurotoxicity risk factor toggle
  • Age-in-hours calculation from birth
  • Rate-of-rise calculation when two measurements are available

Treatment band classification

  • Below phototherapy threshold
  • At/above phototherapy threshold
  • At/above exchange transfusion threshold

Interactive Nomogram

  • Dynamic plotting of bilirubin values
  • Visual comparison with treatment thresholds
  • Horizontal scrolling and zoom
  • Measurement callouts with age, value, and date
  • Clear differentiation of phototherapy and exchange levels

Patient Tracking (Pro)

  • Create and manage patient profiles
  • Store multiple bilirubin measurements
  • Automatic recalculation of treatment classification
  • Longitudinal trend visualization
  • Clinical notes support

Professional PDF Reports (Pro)

  • Patient demographics and measurement summary
  • Risk assessment classification and threshold comparison
  • Nomogram visualization
  • References and disclaimer section
  • Secure export through share sheet

Offline & Private

  • No backend servers
  • No cloud dependency
  • All data stored locally on device
  • Designed for clinical environments
