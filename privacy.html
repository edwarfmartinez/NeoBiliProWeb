Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how NeoBili Pro ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

NeoBili Pro is designed with privacy in mind and operates entirely offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or patient data.

Data Storage

All data entered into the app, including patient information and clinical measurements, is stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third parties.

No Internet Connectivity Required

NeoBili Pro functions completely offline and does not require or use internet connectivity. No data is sent to or received from external servers.

Third-Party Services

The app may link to external services (such as the App Store) for updates or support, but these interactions are subject to the respective service's privacy policies, not ours.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in app updates.
